Who i am

My name is Ranny Kaddoura, I am Syrian, but I was born in Tripoli, Libya in 1980. I have been raised, grew up, and studied in Damascus since I was two months old. I studied electrical engineering at the University of Damascus and worked in the field of informatics for many years. In 2011, the political problems in Syria began. I remained there until the beginning of 2013, trying to cope with the situation, but the Libyan nationality I was carrying was enough to make me a stranger in my country. Then I had to search for a safe place where I could start from scratch a new life again . I am now living in Switzerland Zürich since March 2013 in a place i love called Niederweningen it is my new Hometown now.
